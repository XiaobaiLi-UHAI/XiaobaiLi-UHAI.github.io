About Me

I am currently a ZJU100 professor at the School of Cyber Science and Technology, Zhejiang University, and an adjunct professor at the Center for Machine Vision and Signal Analysis, University of Oulu, Finland. My research focuses on computer vision and machine learning, with special emphasis on facial expression recognition, micro-expression recognition, remote physiological signal measurement, and other applications in affective computing, healthcare, biometrics, and Deepfake detection.
